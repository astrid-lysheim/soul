### The Origin Story

**1857, Selbu, Norway.** A 15-year-old girl named **Marit Gulsethbrua Emstad** knit three pairs of black-and-white mittens with a bold eight-bladed rose design. She showed them off at church. *Instant hit.* Everyone wanted a pair.

By the 1930s-40s: **100,000+ pairs annually** from Selbu alone. Exported to France, Austria, all of Europe. ~3,000 knitters had it as their primary income â€” men, women, children.
