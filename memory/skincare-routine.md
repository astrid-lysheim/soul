## Morning Routine (Every Day)

1. CeraVe Foaming Cleanser (gentle, gets you niacinamide)
2. Neutrogena Hydro Boost on damp skin
3. Sunscreen SPF 50+

☀️ Reapply sunscreen every 2-3h outdoors (CDMX 2,240m altitude + tret = extreme UV sensitivity)

## Evening Routine — Skin Cycling

### Active Nights (Monday & Thursday) — Tretinoin
1. CeraVe **Foaming** Cleanser (mild — NO SA on tret nights!)
2. Neutrogena Hydro Boost (let dry completely)
3. Tretinoin (pea-sized amount)
4. Neutrogena Hydro Boost again (sandwich method)
5. Lubriderm Clinical Therapy to seal

### Recovery Nights (Tue/Wed/Fri/Sat/Sun)
1. CeraVe **SA Renewing** Cleanser (salicylic acid OK on recovery nights)
2. Neutrogena Hydro Boost on damp skin
3. Lubriderm Clinical Therapy to seal
4. No actives tonight

### Sunday — Skin Check
Evaluate: if peeling or red, skip Monday's tret.

### ⚠️ Common Mistake (caught Mar 15)
José was double-cleansing with the SA cleanser morning + night every day. WAY too much exfoliation. Key rules:
- **Morning = CeraVe Foaming only** (mild)
- **SA = evening only, recovery nights only**
- **Never SA + tret on the same night**
- **One cleanser per wash** — no double cleansing without an oil cleanser

## CDMX-Specific Notes
- Altitude 2,240m = ~22% more UV than sea level
- Dry season (Nov-Apr) = low humidity → Hydro Boost alone can dehydrate (needs occlusive seal!)
- PM2.5 pollution = double cleansing is mandatory, not optional
- Oil cleanse removes particulate matter that settles into pores
