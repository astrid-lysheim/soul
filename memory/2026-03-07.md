## Reed — Feature Requests from Miguel Raggi (UNAM tester)

Miguel is a real user reading fantasy (Way of Kings). Three feature requests:

1. **Long word slow-down** — words > ~8 chars get bonus display time proportional to length. Fantasy names like "Naelaryn" pass too fast. Easy win, could be a setting slider.
2. **Scene break pauses** — no pause between scene changes. Sanderson uses decorative images (`<p class="calibre22"><img/></p>`) as scene breaks, followed by `calibre11` (first paragraph of new scene). Detect image tag or calibre11-after-calibre22 → 2-3s pause.
3. **Dialogue font change** — words in `"..."` quotes should render italic or tinted. Pre-parse quote spans at tokenization time, tag each word. Runtime display just checks the flag.

**Epub structure findings (Way of Kings):**
- Scene breaks = `<p class="calibre22"><img src="images/00XXX.jpg"/></p>` followed by `<p class="calibre11">`
- `calibre12` = normal indented paragraph
- `calibre11` = first paragraph of new scene (no indent, 3em top margin)
- `calibre25` = blockquote (in-world epigraphs)
- Dialogue = plain `"..."` quotes, no special HTML tag
- Internal thoughts = `<span class="italic">`
